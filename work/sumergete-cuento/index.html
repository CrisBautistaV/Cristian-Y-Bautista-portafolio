Súmérgete en el cuento

Interactive 8D (ambisonic) audio experience driven by movement and perception

An immersive audiovisual experience where sound responds dynamically to the listener’s movement. Using spatial audio techniques and real-time input, the system creates a narrative environment that evolves through interaction.

Status: finished

Motive

Traditional listening experiences are static and linear. This project explores how narrative and sound can become interactive, allowing the listener to influence the experience through movement and position.

The goal is to create a sense of immersion where perception is shaped not only by sound design, but by the relationship between the body, space, and story.

System

The system combines motion tracking and spatial audio processing to dynamically modify sound in real time.

  • Unity-based environment for audio and interaction
  • IMU sensors for head tracking
  • Computer vision for positional input
  • 8D / spatial audio processing
  • Real-time mapping between movement and sound

Output

The result is a set of interactive audio pieces where the listener becomes an active participant, navigating and shaping the narrative through movement.

The project includes multiple short stories designed to be experienced through headphones, each exploring different spatial and narrative dynamics.
